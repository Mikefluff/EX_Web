Eurox advantages

Robust structure

Euroxes are issued by the EU company and operations are run by the professional financial team based in the Netherlands. For more security, the backing reserves are held in a European bank.

Easy integration

Eurox can be integrated with merchants, exchanges, and wallets just as easily as Ethereum, Bitcoin or any other cryptocurrency.

Without Boundaries

Euroxes issuance or redemption are smooth processes without liquidity constraints. Users can buy or sell as many Euroxes as they want, quickly and with very low fees.

Based on Blockchain

Eurox is built on the Ethereum platform and tokens are ERC20 compliant and can be stored on Ethereum wallets that support this protocol. Transactions are executed by robust Ethereum network.

Safe and secure

Eurox are uncorrelated to the crypto market fluctuations since the Euro reserves in maintained in a one–to–one ratio, that helps to sustain the market forces.

Digital EUR Equivalent

Eurox token brings the best of two words blockchain technology and FIAT.

Framework

  • Eurox is supported by the Proof of Reserve process. This process is fundamental to maintaining the price­ parity between Eurox in circulation and the underlying fiat currency held in reserves.
  • This system ensures that at any given time the balance of fiat currency held in reserve will be equal to (or greater than) the number of Eurox in circulation.
  • Simple but effective approach for conducting Proof of Reserves significantly reduces our counterparty risk as the custodian of the reserve assets.

Token of the future

  • Eurox can be integrated with merchants, exchanges, and wallets just as easily as Bitcoin or any other cryptocurrencies can be integrated.
  • The Eurox platform offers users the opportunity to view in real-time an audit of all Eurox in circulation. This is possible through the Proof Of Reserve system which confirms that every Eurocoin token in circulation is backed by an equal amount of Euro in reserve.
  • Eurox hedges the digital assets and eliminates the problem of exchange rates fluctuations.

Technical features

Eurox inherit the properties of the Ethereum protocol which include: a decentralized exchange; browser based, open source, wallet encryption; transparency, accountability, multiparty security and reporting functions.

The Ethereum protocol which grants and revokes digital tokens represented as metadata, track and report the circulation of Eurox and enable users to transact and store Eurox and other assets/tokens in several different ways.
